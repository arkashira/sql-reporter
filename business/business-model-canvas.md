## Customer Segments
- Data engineers and analytics teams in medium to large enterprises
- Business users who need self-service SQL query capabilities
- Companies with internal data teams but limited resources for ad-hoc reporting

## Value Propositions
- Empower business users to generate reports and insights without relying on data engineers
- Reduce the workload on data engineers by automating routine reporting tasks
- Provide a user-friendly interface for non-technical users to run SQL queries
- Offer customizable reporting templates and dashboards
- Ensure data security and compliance with enterprise standards

## Channels
- Direct sales to enterprises through a dedicated sales team
- Online marketing campaigns targeting data engineers and analytics teams
- Partnerships with data management and analytics software providers
- Webinars and online demos to showcase the product's capabilities
- Free trial offerings to allow potential customers to test the platform

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated account managers for enterprise customers
- 24/7 customer support via chat, email, and phone
- Regular product updates and feature releases based on user feedback
- Community forums and user groups for peer support and knowledge sharing
- Personalized onboarding and training sessions for new users

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription-based pricing model with monthly and annual plans
  - Basic: $50/user/month (~1,700 THB/user/month)
  - Pro: $100/user/month (~3,400 THB/user/month)
  - Enterprise: Custom pricing based on user count and features
- One-time implementation fees for enterprise customers
- Additional revenue from premium support and consulting services
- Revenue sharing with key partners for referrals and integrations
