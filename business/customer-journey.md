 # Customer-Journey.md

## Aware

### Trigger Event
- Potential customer discovers the sql-reporter platform through marketing channels (SEO, social media, content marketing, etc.) or word-of-mouth.
- Business users encounter difficulties in generating reports and insights due to reliance on data engineers.

### Friction Points
- Lack of awareness about the sql-reporter platform and its capabilities.
- Difficulty in understanding the benefits of using a self-service SQL query platform.

### User Emotions
- Frustration with current reporting process and reliance on data engineers.
- Curiosity about the sql-reporter platform and its potential to streamline the reporting process.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and concise messaging about the benefits of the sql-reporter platform.
- Demonstration of the platform's capabilities through a free trial or case studies.

### Metric
- Number of new visitors to the website.

## Consider

### Trigger Event
- Potential customer visits the sql-reporter website and explores the platform's features.

### Friction Points
- Complexity of the platform and its features may be overwhelming for some users.
- Lack of understanding about how the platform integrates with existing systems.

### User Emotions
- Interest in the platform and its capabilities.
- Skepticism about the platform's ability to meet their specific reporting needs.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and easy-to-understand documentation and tutorials.
- Demonstration of the platform's flexibility and ability to integrate with various data sources.

### Metric
- Number of pageviews on the website.
- Time spent on the website.

## Try

### Trigger Event
- Potential customer signs up for a free trial of the sql-reporter platform.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in setting up the platform and integrating it with existing systems.
- Learning curve associated with using the platform.

### User Emotions
- Excitement about trying out the platform and seeing its capabilities firsthand.
- Frustration if the setup process is complicated or if the platform does not meet their specific reporting needs.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Responsive and helpful customer support during the setup process.
- Easy-to-use interface and intuitive navigation.

### Metric
- Number of free trial sign-ups.
- Number of successful platform setups.

## Adopt

### Trigger Event
- Potential customer decides to purchase the sql-reporter platform after the free trial.

### Friction Points
- Cost of the platform may be a barrier for some users.
- Difficulty in migrating existing reports to the new platform.

### User Emotions
- Satisfaction with the platform's capabilities and ease of use.
- Relief at being able to generate reports and insights independently.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Offering a discount or promotional pricing for new customers.
- Providing a smooth and easy migration process for existing reports.

### Metric
- Number of paid subscriptions.

## Expand

### Trigger Event
- Existing customer expands their use of the sql-reporter platform to additional teams or departments.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in managing multiple users and permissions.
- Lack of scalability as the number of users grows.

### User Emotions
- Satisfaction with the platform's ability to meet their reporting needs.
- Excitement about the potential to expand the platform's use within their organization.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Offering tiered pricing plans to accommodate larger teams or departments.
- Providing additional features and integrations to support expanded use of the platform.

### Metric
- Number of active users per account.
- Number of additional teams or departments using the platform.
