# Partner Integration Roadmap for SQL Reporter

## Target Integrations

| Partner/API                | Free-Tier Limits                     | Integration Effort | Value-Add (User Job Solved)                                   | Affiliate/Revenue-Share |
|----------------------------|--------------------------------------|--------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Tableau**                | Free for 1 user, limited features    | M                  | Enables users to visualize SQL-generated reports seamlessly.   | Yes                      |
| **Looker**                 | Free trial available                  | M                  | Provides advanced analytics capabilities for SQL reports.      | Yes                      |
| **Zapier**                 | 5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month              | S                  | Automates report generation workflows with other apps.        | Yes                      |
| **Google Sheets**          | Free for personal use                | S                  | Allows users to export SQL reports directly to Sheets.        | No                       |
| **Slack**                  | Free for small teams                 | S                  | Facilitates report sharing and alerts in team channels.       | No                       |
| **Airtable**               | Free for 1,200 records               | M                  | Users can manage SQL reports in a collaborative database.     | No                       |
| **Salesforce**             | Free trial available                  | L                  | Integrates SQL insights into CRM workflows for sales teams.   | Yes                      |
| **Microsoft Power BI**     | Free for individual use               | M                  | Enhances reporting capabilities with BI tools.                | Yes                      |

## Rationale
1. **Tableau** and **Looker** are leading BI tools that will enhance the reporting capabilities of SQL Reporter, making it easier for business users to visualize their data without needing data engineering support.
2. **Zapier** offers automation capabilities, allowing users to connect SQL Reporter with various applications, streamlining workflows and increasing productivity.
3. **Google Sheets** and **Airtable** provide familiar environments for business users to manipulate and share data, making SQL reports more accessible.
4. **Slack** integration will improve communication around report generation and insights, fostering collaboration among teams.
5. **Salesforce** and **Microsoft Power BI** are critical for organizations that rely on CRM and BI tools, ensuring that SQL-generated insights are integrated into existing business processes.

## Prioritization
Focus on partners with affiliate/revenue-share opportunities first (Tableau, Looker, Zapier, Salesforce, Microsoft Power BI) to maximize revenue potential while enhancing product value for users.
